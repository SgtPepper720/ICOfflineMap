IC Offline Map

Offline-friendly mapping for inclusive churches.

This web app provides an offline-friendly map of Inclusive Church locations in the UK, where the full map is downloaded to your device (around 100MB).

Church information is based on the Inclusive Church database, which is undergoing updates and cleanup at this time.

To install this to your phone, go to your browser menu and select "Add to Home Screen" (or similar). You can then use the app offline, and refresh the data when you have an internet connection.
